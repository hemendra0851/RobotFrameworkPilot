Kerala govt fixes bottled drinking
water price to 213 per litre

Kerala government has announced a price
ceiling for bottled drinking water being sold
across the state to 213 per litre. The cap
was done after the state recognised bottled
drinking water as an ‘essential commodity’.
"No private bottled drinking water
manufacturers can sell their product at more
than the fixed cap,’ Kerala's Food and Civil
Supplies Minister P Thilothaman said.
